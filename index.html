About SpeckyTec

Hi! I'm Specky — Your Friendly Tech Guide 👋

I started SpeckyTec because I kept seeing friends and family buy the wrong thing — not because they were silly, but because the tech industry makes buying decisions deliberately confusing.

Adverts shout at you. Review sites are full of paid opinions. Shop assistants want to sell you the most expensive option. It's a minefield.

My job is simple: help you cut through all of that and pick the right product for your life, your budget, and your needs.

No jargon. No judgement. Just honest, friendly advice — every single week.
